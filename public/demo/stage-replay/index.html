Demo · Stage Replay

Relive the race through key moments.

The stage replay summarizes what happened during the race: breakaways, splits, attacks, crashes, time gaps and the final sprint or climb. It lets you understand why your tactics succeeded or failed.

Replay visualization

Race detail screen showing route, replay context, standings and race information.

In the full game, each stage replay can show:

  • A timeline of key events with colored segments.
  • Groups and gaps at important points on the route.
  • Attacks from leaders, helpers and rivals.
  • How fatigue and tactics changed the outcome over time.
  • Final classification tables for the stage and general standings.

Learning from each race

Stage replays are not just cosmetic. They help managers understand patterns: which riders struggle in crosswinds, who recovers well, and how different strategies perform in similar races.

Decisions for the next event

After reviewing a replay, you might decide to adjust future race plans, change rider roles, bring in a different sprinter or modify your overall season goals.
