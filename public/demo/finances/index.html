Demo · Finances

Keep your club financially healthy.

The finances section shows how money flows through your club. Salaries, staff, infrastructure, transfers and sponsor deals all affect whether you can sustain success over multiple seasons.

Budget overview

In the real game, the finance dashboard typically includes:

  • Current balance and projected end‑of‑season balance.
  • Income breakdown from sponsors, prizes and transfers.
  • Expense breakdown for wages, staff, facilities and travel.
  • Historical charts that show trends over time.

Financial decisions

Managers use finance data to answer questions like:

  • Can we afford a new leader or should we invest in youth?
  • Is it time to upgrade training facilities?
  • Are wage costs growing faster than income?
  • Do we need to meet certain sponsor objectives to stay solvent?

Risk and stability

Short‑term spending can improve results but also increase risk. The game rewards managers who find a balance between ambition and sustainable budgeting, especially in longer career saves.

Results turn into rankings

Good financial health makes it easier to build a strong squad, which in turn can climb the rankings. The last part of this demo focuses on how performances are reflected in tables and leaderboards.
