Demo · Rankings

See where your club stands.

The rankings view summarizes how your club compares to others over the season. It turns race results, consistency and objectives into clear tables and progress indicators.

Ranking tables

Team ranking screen showing standings, points and season progress.

In the full game, rankings can include:

  • Overall club ranking based on season points.
  • Discipline-specific rankings such as GC, sprint or classics.
  • Rider rankings highlighting individual stars.
  • Historical comparisons with previous seasons.

Connecting systems

Rankings are the visible result of everything you do: race preparation, training, finances, transfers and club strategy. Strong planning leads to stable progress; risky decisions can cause big jumps or sudden drops.

Motivation and goals

Many managers set personal goals around rankings: reaching a higher division, finishing in the top 10 of a specific table, or helping a favorite rider climb to the top of individual rankings.

Experience rankings in the live game

In the real ProPeloton Manager, rankings update after every race and can trigger sponsor reactions, media stories or internal team milestones.
