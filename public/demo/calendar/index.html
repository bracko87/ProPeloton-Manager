Demo · Race Calendar

Plan your season like a real team manager.

The race calendar shows all available events, from small preparation races to prestigious stage races. It helps you choose objectives, rotate riders and manage fatigue over weeks and months.

Calendar at a glance

Calendar screen with race schedule, season planning and upcoming events.

The real calendar groups races by month and highlights key details:

  • Race name, category and terrain profile.
  • Registration deadline and race start date.
  • Icons for stage races, one-day races and time trials.
  • Indicators for races that match your sponsor objectives.

Strategic planning

Managers use the calendar to decide where to attack the season and where to save energy. A strong calendar plan might include:

  • Early-season tune-up races for form building.
  • Targeted stage races for GC or stage wins.
  • Key objectives requested by sponsors.
  • Rest periods after demanding blocks of competition.

Interaction with other systems

The calendar is tightly connected with race preparation, fatigue and finances. Entering more races can bring extra opportunities but also more travel costs and fatigue risk.

Smart managers combine calendar choices with training plans and budget controls to keep the team competitive all year.

Try the live calendar

In the full game, you can filter the calendar by race type, difficulty or objectives and build your own custom plan for the season.
