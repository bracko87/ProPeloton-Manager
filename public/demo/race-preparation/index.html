Demo · Race Preparation

Prepare your squad for each race.

Race preparation is where decisions turn into results. Managers pick the squad, assign roles, choose equipment and define a basic game plan that the race engine will follow.

Preparation screen mockup

Race preparation screen for selecting riders, roles, equipment and tactics.

The real race preparation screen brings together all the required settings:

  • Race details such as terrain, distance and weather.
  • Selected riders with form, fatigue and key stats.
  • Roles and protection for leaders, helpers and sprinters.
  • Equipment and supplies tuned to the race demands.
  • Basic stage plan for aggression, pacing and objectives.

Examples of preparation choices

Managers might decide to:

  • Protect a climber in a mountain stage and sacrifice helpers early.
  • Ride defensively in crosswinds to avoid unnecessary fatigue.
  • Use an aggressive plan in small races to chase stage wins.
  • Focus on top-10 consistency in rankings-relevant events.

Impact on race outcomes

Race preparation does not guarantee a result, but it heavily influences how the simulation behaves. A well-prepared team is more likely to be in the right place at the right time, especially in decisive moments.

See what happens after preparation

Once the start is given, the race engine processes your decisions and generates a full stage with key moments and final results.
