Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for ProPeloton Manager.

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it is used, how the game is operated, and how players can contact us about privacy questions.

Last updated: July 2026

1. Overview

ProPeloton Manager is an online cycling management game operated by Next Quest Studio. We use information to run the game, maintain accounts, process gameplay actions, provide support, protect the service, improve performance, handle payments and comply with platform or legal requirements.

2. Information we collect

We may collect account information such as email address, username, profile details, authentication data and support communication. We may also collect technical information such as browser type, device information, approximate region, session information, error logs and security-related metadata.

Gameplay data can include club details, riders, staff, training, finances, race preparation, race entries, tactics, rankings, notifications, purchases, coin balance, public reviews and other records connected to the account.

3. How we use information

We use information to create and maintain accounts, operate gameplay systems, save progress, show race results, calculate rankings, manage coins, process purchases, provide support, detect abuse, prevent fraud, improve game stability and communicate important service information.

4. Payments and coins

ProPeloton Manager may offer coin packages or paid features. Payment processing is handled by external payment providers such as Stripe. We do not store full payment card details on our own servers. We may store transaction references, purchase history, coin ledger entries and refund-related records needed to operate the game.

5. Advertising and rewarded ads

The game may use advertising services, including Google advertising products, to show ads or rewarded ads. Rewarded ads may allow players to receive in-game coins or progress after voluntarily watching an ad.

Advertising partners may use cookies, device identifiers, consent signals and similar technologies to provide, measure and improve advertising. In regions where consent is required, a consent message may be shown before personalized advertising is used.

6. Cookies and browser storage

We may use cookies, browser storage and similar technologies for login, session handling, preferences, security, analytics, advertising consent and game functionality. Some features may not work correctly if required storage is disabled.

7. Support and communication

If you contact support, we may use the information you provide to understand the issue, answer your question, investigate bugs and improve the service. Do not send passwords, full payment card details or private authentication codes.

8. Sharing information

We may share limited information with service providers that help operate the game, including hosting, database, authentication, payment processing, analytics, advertising and support tools. We do not sell player account information as a standalone product.

9. Data security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect account and game data. No online service can guarantee perfect security, but we work to keep the game reliable and safe.

10. Data retention

We may keep account, gameplay, payment, support and security records for as long as needed to operate the game, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent abuse and maintain accurate game history.

11. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at contact@propelotonmanager.com.
