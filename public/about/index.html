About ProPeloton Manager

Build your cycling legacy.

ProPeloton Manager is an online cycling management game where players create and develop a cycling club, prepare race calendars, manage riders, handle finances, negotiate transfers, follow rankings and compete across a long season-based cycling world.

What the game is about

ProPeloton Manager is designed for players who enjoy cycling, strategy, long-term planning and multiplayer competition. Instead of controlling a rider directly, you act as the manager of a cycling club. Your job is to build a balanced squad, prepare races, protect finances, develop riders, handle support staff, improve equipment and make decisions that affect the full season.

The game world includes race calendars, team rankings, race preparation, stage plans, equipment, training, staff, sponsors, transfers, financial management, infrastructure and developing-team systems. Every club has to balance ambition with long-term stability.

Team management

Build your squad with sprinters, climbers, time-trial riders, helpers, leaders and young riders. Contracts, morale, fatigue, potential and development all matter.

Race preparation

Apply for races, select riders, assign staff and assets, choose equipment, prepare supplies and create stage plans before deadlines.

Season progress

Teams earn points, climb rankings, manage sponsors, pay salaries and plan for promotion, relegation and long-term club growth.

Why the public website exists

Visitors should be able to understand the game before creating an account. That is why the public site includes an overview of the game, instructions on how to play, support information, contact details, a privacy policy and terms of use.
