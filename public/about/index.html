About ProPeloton Manager

A cycling manager game by Next Quest Studio.

ProPeloton Manager is an online cycling management game where players create, develop and guide a cycling club through a long season of decisions, race preparation, team building, financial planning and competition.

About Next Quest Studio

Next Quest Studio is a small independent developer company building ProPeloton Manager. Our goal is to create a detailed, readable and fair cycling management experience for players who enjoy long-term strategy, sports simulation and team development.

What ProPeloton Manager is

ProPeloton Manager is built around the idea that cycling is more than race day. A successful team depends on scouting, rider development, contracts, finances, race selection, staff, equipment, recovery, tactics and planning. The game gives players control over those decisions in a persistent cycling world.

Instead of controlling a rider directly, you act as the manager of a cycling club. You decide how the club grows, which riders fit your team, which races match your squad, how much risk you take in the market and how carefully you protect the budget.

Sporting strategy

Riders have different strengths. Sprinters, climbers, time-trial riders, rouleurs, helpers and leaders all matter depending on the race profile, weather, team role and season objective.

Management depth

Club decisions include finances, salaries, sponsors, transfers, race applications, preparation deadlines, equipment, support staff and long-term infrastructure.

Season progress

Results and rankings create long-term goals. Managers must think beyond one stage and plan for the future of the club across the full season.

Why we are building it

Cycling has many layers that are interesting for management gameplay: team roles, fatigue, calendar planning, race profiles, transfers, development, sponsorship and financial discipline. ProPeloton Manager brings those layers together in one online game.

The public website exists so visitors can understand the game before registration. You can read about the concept, learn how to play, contact support, review privacy information and understand the terms before creating an account.
