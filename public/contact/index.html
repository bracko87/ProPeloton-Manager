Contact

Contact ProPeloton Manager.

Use this page for general questions, support requests, bug reports, account questions, privacy questions and feedback about ProPeloton Manager.

Contact email

You can contact ProPeloton Manager by email. Helpful messages include the page name, team name, race name, rider name, expected behavior, actual behavior and screenshots when possible.

contact@propelotonmanager.com

Support requests

For account, login, payment, coin or gameplay problems, include your account email and enough detail for support to understand the issue.

Bug reports

For bugs, describe what you were trying to do, what happened, what should have happened, and whether the issue can be repeated.

Privacy and sensitive information

Do not send passwords, full payment card details or private authentication codes. For privacy questions, include the email address connected to your account and a clear description of your request.
