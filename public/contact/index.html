Contact ProPeloton Manager

Need help or want to report a problem?

Use this page for support requests, bug reports, account questions, gameplay feedback, privacy questions, payment or coin questions, and general contact about ProPeloton Manager.

Support email

You can also contact us directly at:

contact@propelotonmanager.com

For account, privacy, payment or rewarded-ad questions, include the email address connected to your game account. Do not send passwords or full payment card details.

Good bug reports include

  • The page where the issue happened.
  • Your team name, race name or rider name if relevant.
  • What you expected to happen.
  • What actually happened.
  • Screenshots or copied error messages if available.

Send a message

Fill out the form below. Your message will be sent directly to ProPeloton Manager support. Your email app will not open.

Please do not include passwords, full payment card details, private authentication codes or secret keys.

What happens after you send a message?

We receive your message through the contact service. If a reply is needed, we will answer using the email address you provided. Clear reports with page names, screenshots and exact error messages are much easier to investigate.
